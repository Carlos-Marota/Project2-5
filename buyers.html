Buyers requirements

They are some requirements that is needed for buyers here are six requirements that are needed Down payment, A lender, Credit scores, Debt-to-income ratio, Closing costs, Documentation.

Down payment- Down payment should be first requirement to but a home because this is the money to pay upfront to set amount money need to borrow. The amount you need for down payment depends on the type of loan you are going get as an exampke for FHA mortgages need at least 3.5%.

A lender- Chosing a lender is very important so chose your lender wise and look around. The reason why it important it can influence your interest rate you pay for mortgage loan the lower rate the less you gonna pay for the home over the life of the loan.

Credit scored- Getting your credit scored decide if you qualify for a mortgage. They check everything to see how likely you pay what you have borrow. Every loan officer have different credit score requirement most loaners want to works with 620 or higher. Is not unheard of that loaner will work with as low as 500 but it be hard to find.

Debt to income ratio- This represent the percentage of your income that will go toward to debt repay ent each month. The lower the number is, the easier it be to meet requirements to buy a house.The bank use this to see you can afford a home. If you want to figure this out yourself you can add all your monthly deby payments and divide it by your gross income each month. You can reduce it by paying more each month or earn more money.

Closing costs- These are the fees and chargers you pay at closing, if these arent payed then the loan documentation and mortgage process won't be signed or complete. Some fees are: Credit report, Application, Recording, Title insurance. Closing, Escrow, Origination, Prepaid interest, Underwriting or processing, and Survey. Your lender can provide estimate what you pay at closing an average closing cost run from 2% to 5% and nowdays prices in la country be at least to $700,000 for house and be paying at least $14,000 to $35,000 at closing.

Documentation- It important to much you have all Documentations ready because if not ready it can delay or even stop the loan approval process altogether but if you have loaner already they will have full checklist what documentation you need and will make sure you got everything. A pre-approval is preliminary based on what borrower stated on the application such as income, debt, asserts, and employment among others.To be approval need provide pay stubs, tax returns, back statements, W2 and employment verifications.
